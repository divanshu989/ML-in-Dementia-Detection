OASIS Brains Dataset Exploration

The OASIS (Open Access Series of Imaging Studies) Brains dataset represents a comprehensive longitudinal neuroimaging research collection. This groundbreaking dataset provides unprecedented insights into brain aging and neurological changes, focusing on subjects aged 60-96 with multiple MRI scans per individual.

Dataset Highlights

  • 373 Subjects Studied
  • Age Range: 60-96 Years
  • Multiple Scans per Subject

Research Categories

  • • Cognitive Decline Assessment
  • • Neurological Aging
  • • Brain Volume Changes
  • • Dementia Progression
  • • Neuroplasticity Studies
