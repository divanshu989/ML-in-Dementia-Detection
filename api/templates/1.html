Introduction to HealthCare - Detection of Dementia

What are we trying to solve:

Dementia is a complex neurological condition characterized by a decline in mental ability severe enough to interfere with daily life. It's not a specific disease, but an umbrella term describing a group of symptoms associated with memory loss and reduced cognitive function. Our goal is to develop a robust machine learning model that enables clinicians to detect dementia proactively, transforming early intervention strategies.
